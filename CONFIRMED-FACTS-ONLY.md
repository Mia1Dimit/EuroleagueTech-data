## ⚠️ WHAT IS NOT CONFIRMED

### Market Adoption Rates
- **No Euroleague team publicly discloses** complete technology vendor lists
- **Adoption percentages** (e.g., "67% use Synergy") are **industry estimates**, not confirmed data
- **Specific team budgets** are not publicly disclosed

### Pricing
- **No vendors publicly disclose** Euroleague-specific contract values
- Pricing ranges cited in research are **industry estimates** based on:
  - Public pricing for smaller organizations
  - General market positioning
  - Vendor tier positioning (budget vs. premium)
- **Actual contracts vary** significantly by team, features, contract length

### ROI Claims
- **Performance improvements** attributed to technology are difficult to isolate
- **Revenue increases** (ticket sales, merchandise) have multiple contributing factors
- **Injury prevention savings** are theoretical calculations, not audited results

### "Likely Adopters" Lists
- **Speculation** based on budget estimates, technology needs, vendor geography
- **Not confirmations** - teams rarely announce full technology stacks publicly
