# Euroleague-Tech-data

Structured research dataset for Euroleague basketball technology landscape analysis.

## What This Repository Contains

This repository organizes public research into reusable markdown datasets across:
- team technology profiles
- vendor profiles
- team staff profiles
- technology domain analyses
- research reports and references

## Repository Structure

- `euroleague-teams/`: team-level technology snapshots
- `vendor-profiles/`: vendor capability and positioning profiles
- `euroleague-teams-staff/`: staff and performance role mappings
- `technology-analysis/`: deep-dive analysis by domain
- `research-reports/`: selected research evidence and references
- `euroleague-official-docs/`: placeholders and references to official documents

## Data Principles

- Facts are separated from assumptions where possible.
- Sources are public and traceable whenever available.
- This is a research dataset, not legal/financial advice.

## Legal and Usage Notes

- Team names, logos, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

## What Is Intentionally Excluded from Public Release
