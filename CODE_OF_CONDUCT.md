# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on evidence and constructive feedback.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Offensive or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies to all project interactions, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject content that violates this Code of Conduct.
