## Product Portfolio

### KINEXON PERFORM LPS
- **Type**: Local Positioning System
- **Description**: Fixed sensor network for real-time 3D tracking
- **Data Collected**: X, Y, Z coordinates, speed, distance, player positioning
- **Accuracy**: Centimeter-level precision, sub-second latency
- **Use Case**: Real-time training and game monitoring

### KINEXON PERFORM IMU
- **Type**: Wearable Inertial Measurement Unit
- **Description**: Ultra-light sensors worn on waistband
- **Data Collected**: Acceleration, deceleration, jumps, changes of direction, heart rate, load
- **Sampling Rate**: 100+ Hz
- **Use Case**: Daily load management, injury prevention

### KINEXON COMPETE Vision
- **Type**: AI-powered video analysis
- **Description**: Computer vision for automatic shot tracking and analysis
- **Capabilities**: Counts all shots, tracks location, analyzes success rate, identifies contested vs. open shots
- **Integration**: Works with PERFORM systems for complete analytics
- **Launch**: 2024-25 season
