## Product Portfolio

### STATSports APEX

**APEX Athlete Series** (Current Flagship)
- **Type**: GPS + IMU wearable pod
- **Form Factor**: Vest-mounted pod (10-12 hour battery)
- **Data Collected**: GPS positioning, speed, distance, accelerations, heart rate, dynamic stress load
- **Sampling Rate**: 10Hz GPS, 1000Hz IMU
- **Key Metric**: Dynamic Stress Load (DSL) - proprietary load metric
- **Sports**: Soccer, rugby, American football, basketball (limited)
- **Launch**: 2020 platform, iterative updates

**APEX Pro Series**
- **Type**: Premium 18Hz GPS variant
- **Target**: Elite professional teams
- **Differentiation**: Higher sampling rate for faster movements
- **Use case**: Top-tier soccer, rugby primarily

**APEX Indoor** (Basketball Relevant)
- **Type**: Indoor-optimized pod (reduces GPS dependency)
- **Technology**: IMU-heavy analysis when GPS signal weak/absent
- **Limitation**: Not true indoor positioning like KINEXON LPS or Catapult T7
- **Workaround**: Relies on accelerometer data when indoors, loses positional tracking

### Sonra Platform

**Description**: Cloud-based analytics and athlete management  
**Features**:
- Real-time data dashboards
- Athlete wellness monitoring
- Session planning and periodization
- Benchmarking across positions
- Mobile app for athlete check-ins

**Integration**: Syncs with APEX hardware, third-party video (limited)

## Technology Differentiation

### Unique Features
- **Dynamic Stress Load (DSL)**: Proprietary load metric combining GPS, IMU, and HR data
- **User experience**: Noted for intuitive interface and ease of use
- **Soccer optimization**: Algorithms tuned specifically for football/soccer movements
- **Real-time capability**: Live data streaming during training
- **Rugged hardware**: Durable pods built for contact sports

### Integration Capabilities
- **Video platforms**: Limited (not core strength like Catapult)
- **Third-party HR**: Compatible with major heart rate strap manufacturers
- **APIs**: Basic data export capabilities
- **Cloud platform**: Sonra web and mobile access

### Data Types Collected
- **GPS (outdoor)**: Positioning, speed zones, distance, high-speed running
- **IMU (indoor/outdoor)**: Accelerations, decelerations, changes of direction, jumps
- **Load**: Dynamic Stress Load, high metabolic load distance
- **Physiological**: Heart rate, heart rate zones
- **Wellness**: Subjective athlete reporting (sleep, soreness, mood)

**Basketball Limitation**: No accurate indoor positioning - critical gap for basketball

- **Outdoor training focus**: Teams doing significant outdoor conditioning
- **Budget constraint**: Teams with <€30k/year performance tech budget

### Honest Assessment
**STATSports is world-class for soccer but not well-suited for professional basketball** due to indoor positioning limitations. Euroleague teams almost always choose KINEXON, Catapult, or WIMU PRO instead.
