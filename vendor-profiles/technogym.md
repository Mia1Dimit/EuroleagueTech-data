## Company Overview

**Technogym** is the **global leader in fitness and wellness equipment** for professional sports, gyms, hotels, and homes. Founded by Nerio Alessandri in 1983 in Cesena (near Bologna), Italy, Technogym has become the **official supplier to 8 Olympic Games** (Sydney 2000 through Paris 2024) and serves elite sports teams across multiple disciplines including basketball, soccer, American football, and Formula 1.

### Market Position

**Professional Sports Market Leader**:
- **200+ professional sports teams** globally (soccer, basketball, rugby, F1)
- Official supplier: Italian National Teams (soccer, basketball, volleyball, etc.)
- NBA presence: Multiple teams use Technogym equipment
- **European basketball**: Confirmed partnerships with at least 2 Euroleague teams

**Euroleague Adoption**: **2 confirmed installations**
1. **Virtus Segafredo Bologna** - Exercise Technology Partner
2. **EA7 Olimpia Milano** - Official Sponsor

### 2. Cardio Equipment

**SKILLRUN** - Professional Treadmill:
- Designed for sports performance training
- Includes sled mode for resistance running
- Speed/power output tracking

**SKILLROW** - Rowing Ergometer:
- Performance rowing machine
- Power output tracking
- Training programs for athletes

### 3. Functional Training

**SKILLTOOLS**:
- Battle ropes, slam balls, medicine balls, TRX stations
- Professional-grade functional equipment
- Modular training stations

**KINESIS** - Cable-based functional trainer:
- 3D movement patterns
- Adaptive resistance
- Sport-specific training

### 4. Digital & Connected Solutions

**mywellness Platform**:
- Cloud-based training management
- Exercise prescription and tracking
- Performance data aggregation
- Integration with wearables and apps
- **Team dashboard** for coaches/trainers to monitor athletes

**Technogym Live** (Commercial Gyms):
- Entertainment system for cardio equipment
- Not typically used in professional sports

### 5. Assessment & Testing

**CHECKUP** - Fitness Assessment Station:
- Strength testing (grip, leg press, etc.)
- Flexibility assessment
- Body composition (when paired with scales)
