# Vendor Profile: Isokinetic

## Company Overview

**Isokinetic** is a **premier sports medicine and rehabilitation center group** founded in 1996 in Bologna, Italy. The organization operates a **network of medical centers** specializing in **injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance optimization** for professional athletes. Isokinetic is known for serving **elite European soccer clubs**, international athletes, and increasingly **Euroleague basketball teams**.

### Market Position

**European Sports Medicine Leader**:
- **FIFA Medical Centre of Excellence** (accredited by FIFA)
- **Official medical partner** for numerous Serie A soccer clubs
- **Medical staff**: 100+ specialists (orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, sports physicians, biomechanics experts)
- **Facilities**: 10+ centers across Italy (Bologna flagship + Milan, Rome, Turin, etc.)

**Euroleague Adoption**: **1 confirmed partnership**
1. **Virtus Segafredo Bologna** - Medical & Rehabilitation Partner

**Service Philosophy**: **Evidence-based sports medicine** combining clinical care, biomechanics research, and performance science.

## Service Portfolio

### 1. Injury Rehabilitation (Core Service)

**Post-Surgery Rehabilitation**:
- ACL reconstruction rehab (common in basketball - knee injuries)
- Meniscus repair protocols
- Shoulder surgery rehab (post-op rotator cuff, labrum)
- Ankle reconstruction rehab

**Conservative Treatment** (Non-Surgical):
- Muscle strains/tears rehabilitation
- Tendinopathy treatment (patellar tendon, Achilles)
- Back pain management
- Acute injury rehab (sprains, contusions)

**Return-to-Play Protocols**:
- Functional testing before clearance
- Sport-specific training progression
- Load management integration
- Medical clearance coordination

### 2. Injury Prevention

**Screening & Assessment**:
- **Biomechanical analysis**: Motion capture for injury risk (jumping, landing, cutting)
- **Isokinetic testing**: Muscle strength/imbalance assessment (Cybex/Biodex dynamometers)
- **Functional movement screening**: Identify movement dysfunctions
- **Physical profiling**: Baseline data for players (strength, power, flexibility, balance)

**Prevention Programs**:
- Neuromuscular training (balance, proprioception, landing mechanics)
- Strength training prescription (address imbalances)
- Monitoring recommendations (load management, fatigue tracking)

### 3. Performance Optimization

**Biomechanics Lab**:
- Motion capture analysis (3D kinematics)
- Force plate testing (jump height, power output, asymmetries)
- Gait analysis (running mechanics)
- Sport-specific movement assessment (basketball: shooting form, landing, cutting)

**Medical Performance Services**:
- VO2 max testing (aerobic capacity)
- Lactate threshold testing
- Body composition analysis (DEXA scans)
- Nutritional consultation (collaboration with dietitians)

### 4. Medical Imaging & Diagnostics

**On-Site Diagnostics**:
- MRI (musculoskeletal imaging)
- Ultrasound (soft tissue, real-time diagnostics)
- X-ray (bone injuries, joint assessment)
- Blood testing (biomarkers, nutritional status)

**Diagnostic Speed**:
- Same-day imaging availability
- Rapid diagnosis for acute injuries
- Coordinate with team physicians for treatment decisions

### 5. Surgery & Orthopedic Care

**Surgical Team**:
- Orthopedic surgeons on staff (knee, shoulder, ankle specialists)
- Arthroscopic surgery capabilities
- Partnerships with hospitals for major surgeries

**Post-Op Integration**:
- Seamless transition from surgery → rehab (same facility)
- Surgeon-physiotherapist collaboration on protocols

## Euroleague Use Case: Virtus Bologna

### Partnership Structure

**Designation**: "Medical & Rehabilitation Partner"

**Services Provided**:
1. **Injury rehabilitation**: Virtus players with injuries go to Isokinetic for rehab
2. **Prevention screening**: Pre-season/mid-season biomechanical assessments
3. **Medical imaging**: MRI/ultrasound for injury diagnosis
4. **Performance testing**: Baseline fitness testing, return-to-play validation
5. **Medical consultation**: Orthopedic specialists advise team doctors

**Integration with Team Medical Staff**:
- **Virtus team doctors** remain primary medical authority
- Isokinetic provides **specialized services** (biomechanics, imaging, rehab)
- **Referral model**: Team sends players to Isokinetic for specific services
- **Data sharing**: Isokinetic reports back to team medical staff

**Physical Proximity Advantage**:
- Isokinetic flagship center is **in Bologna** (same city as Virtus)
- Players can visit Isokinetic for rehab sessions while staying in Bologna
- No travel required (vs. sending players to Milan, Rome, etc.)

### Example Use Cases

**Scenario 1: ACL Injury**
- Player tears ACL during game
- **Isokinetic MRI** (next day) confirms diagnosis
- **Isokinetic orthopedic surgeon** performs ACL reconstruction
- **Isokinetic rehab team** manages 6-9 month rehab protocol
- **Return-to-play testing** at Isokinetic (biomechanics, strength, functional tests)
- **Medical clearance** coordinated between Isokinetic surgeon and Virtus team doctor

**Scenario 2: Injury Prevention**
- Pre-season: All Virtus players visit Isokinetic for **biomechanical screening**
- **Motion capture analysis** identifies landing asymmetries (ACL risk)
- **Isokinetic testing** reveals quad/hamstring imbalance (injury risk)
- **Prevention program** prescribed (neuromuscular training, strength work)
- **Mid-season re-test** to track progress, adjust program

**Scenario 3: Minor Muscle Strain**
- Player has hamstring strain (Grade 1)
- **Isokinetic ultrasound** confirms diagnosis, severity
- **Rehab protocol** at Isokinetic facility (3-4 weeks)
- **Return-to-play testing** (strength, flexibility, functional movement)
- **Cleared** to return to Virtus training

## Technology & Equipment

### Biomechanics Lab

**Motion Capture**:
- Multi-camera 3D motion analysis (Vicon, OptiTrack, or similar)
- Marker-based or markerless tracking
- Captures joint angles, velocities, accelerations during movement

**Force Plates**:
- Ground reaction force measurement
- Jump testing (height, power, asymmetries)
- Balance/stability assessment

**Isokinetic Dynamometers**:
- **Cybex** or **Biodex** equipment
- Measure muscle strength at controlled speeds
- Identify strength imbalances (quad/hamstring ratios, left/right differences)

### Medical Imaging

**MRI**:
- High-field scanners (1.5T or 3T)
- Musculoskeletal protocols (knee, shoulder, ankle, spine)
- Fast results (same-day or next-day interpretations)

**Ultrasound**:
- Real-time soft tissue imaging
- Muscle, tendon, ligament visualization
- Portable scanners for clinic or pitch-side use

**X-Ray**:
- Standard radiography for bone injuries
- Joint alignment assessment

### Rehabilitation Equipment

**Therapeutic Modalities**:
- Laser therapy, ultrasound therapy, electrical stimulation
- Cryotherapy, heat therapy
- Manual therapy tables, treatment beds

**Exercise Equipment**:
- Reformer Pilates for core/stability
- TRX, resistance bands, medicine balls for functional training
- AlterG anti-gravity treadmill (if available) for low-impact running
- Strength equipment for progressive loading
