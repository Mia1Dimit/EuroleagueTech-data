## Product Portfolio

### ArtemYs - AI-Powered Biomechanical Analysis Platform

**Type**: Wearable IMU-based performance monitoring with myoskeletal analysis

**Core Technology**:
- **IMU Sensors**: High-performance accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers
- **Sampling Rate**: 200 Hz for precise, high-resolution analysis
- **Detection Range**: Up to 15g (captures explosive movements)
- **Form Factor**: Among the smallest sensors on the market for maximum athlete comfort
- **Portability**: Compact design with dedicated travel case

**Key Capabilities**:
1. **Personalized Workload Monitoring**
   - Athlete-specific workload zones (not generic one-size-fits-all)
   - AI-generated Ground Reaction Forces (GRFs)
   - Advanced engineering metrics

2. **Myoskeletal Analysis** (Unique Differentiator)
   - **Muscle-specific stress load tracking** for major muscle groups:
     - Quadriceps Femoris (L/R)
     - Biceps Femoris (L/R)
     - Rectus Femoris (L/R)
     - Semitendinosus (L/R)
     - Adductors (L/R)
     - Gastrocnemius (L/R)
     - Soleus (L/R)
   - **Muscle-specific capacity monitoring** (e.g., 96% capacity = elevated fatigue risk)
   - **Early detection alerts** for excessive muscle stress before injury occurs

3. **Predictive Analytics**
   - **5+ day workload forecasting** (first tool on the market with this capability)
   - Stress load prediction
   - Muscle deformation prediction
   - Asymmetry detection (bilateral imbalances)

4. **Digital Twin & Athlete ID**
   - Personalized biomechanical model for each athlete
   - MRI bioengineering analysis integration (Pro/Elite tiers)
   - Complete athlete medical ID with historical data

**Data Processing**:
- 3D sensor data from industry-standard tracking devices
- AI algorithms create personalized "digital twin" for each athlete
- Seamless monitoring dashboard with real-time alerts
- Mobile apps (iOS and Android) for on-the-go access

## Technology Differentiation

### Unique Selling Propositions

1. **First-to-Market Myoskeletal Analysis**
   - **Industry-leading innovation**: Only solution detecting muscle-specific stress loads
   - **Preventive focus**: Identifies micro-failures before they become major injuries
   - **Precision**: Muscle-by-muscle capacity tracking (not just global load)

2. **Personalized Workload Zones**
   - **Athlete-specific thresholds** (vs. generic population norms)
   - **Dynamic adjustment** as athlete's fitness evolves
   - **Robust assessment** validated by engineering principles

3. **5-Day Predictive Window**
   - **Market first**: Only tool forecasting 5+ days of workload/stress
   - **Proactive planning**: Coaches can adjust training before fatigue accumulates
   - **Travel/competition planning**: Critical for Euroleague's packed schedules

4. **Simplicity & Ease of Use**
   - **Minimal setup**: Requires only tracking sensor data (no complex installations)
   - **Immediate insights**: Actionable data without engineering PhD required
   - **Mobile-first**: Athletes and staff access data via smartphone apps

5. **Engineering Rigor**
   - **BioEngineering foundation**: PhD-level expertise in computational mechanics
   - **Academic validation**: "EVO Labs" research division publishes papers
   - **Multi-disciplinary**: Combines CFD, FEA, material science, deep learning
