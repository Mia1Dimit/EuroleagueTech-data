# Vendor Profile: Sixtus

**Category**: Sports Nutrition & Recovery Products  
**Headquarters**: Pogliano Milanese, Italy (near Milan)  
**Founded**: 1946  
**Market Focus**: Professional Sports Nutrition (Italy & Europe)  
**Last Updated**: March 6, 2026

---

## Company Overview

**Sixtus** is an **Italian sports nutrition and recovery products manufacturer** with nearly **80 years of history** serving professional athletes. Founded in 1946 in Pogliano Milanese (Milan metropolitan area), Sixtus specializes in **topical recovery products** (massage oils, muscle creams, cold/heat therapy gels) and **nutritional supplements** for elite sports teams.

### Market Position

**Italian Professional Sports Leader**:
- **Dominant in Italian cycling** (Giro d'Italia teams)
- **Strong presence in Italian soccer** (Serie A clubs)
- **Basketball adoption**: Confirmed partnerships with 2 Euroleague teams
- **Regional focus**: 80% of business in Italy, expanding to broader Europe

**Euroleague Adoption**: **2 confirmed partnerships**
1. **Virtus Segafredo Bologna** - Nutrition & Recovery Partner
2. **EA7 Olimpia Milano** - Official Supplier

**Product Philosophy**: **Professional-grade formulations** for athletes, not consumer/retail focus

## Product Portfolio

### 1. Topical Recovery Products (Core Business)

**Massage & Muscle Care**:
- **Arnica Forte** - Post-exercise muscle relief gel (arnica + menthol)
- **Deocontract Gel** - Muscle relaxant for tight/overworked muscles
- **Defaticante** - Recovery spray for post-game muscle fatigue
- **Sport Oil** - Massage oil for pre-game preparation
- **Forte Oil** - Deep tissue massage oil

**Cold/Heat Therapy**:
- **Ghiaccio Spray** - Instant ice spray for acute injuries
- **Thermo** - Warming cream for muscle preparation
- **Criogel** - Cold therapy gel (alternative to ice)

**Skin Protection**:
- **Chamois Cream** - Anti-chafing (cycling focus)
- **Warming/Cooling Balms** - Pre-competition muscle preparation

### 2. Nutritional Supplements

**Energy & Performance**:
- Energy gels (cycling/endurance focus)

- Post-workout recovery drinks

**NOTE**: Supplement portfolio is smaller/less prominent than topical products (not primary focus)

### 3. Hydration Solutions

- Sports drink mixes
- Electrolyte tablets

## Pricing & ROI

### Estimated Investment

**Annual Supply Contract** (Euroleague team):
- **Product volume**: 100-200 units/month (gels, oils, sprays, creams)
- **Cost per unit**: €15-40 (professional-grade products)
- **Monthly cost**: €2,000-€6,000
- **Annual cost**: €24,000-€72,000

**Sponsorship Model** (Official Supplier designation):
- Products provided at **discount or free** in exchange for:
  - Brand visibility (logo in physio room, social media mentions)
  - Testimonials from medical staff
  - Access to team for product testing
- **Estimated value exchange**: €30-60k/year (products + services)

### ROI for Teams

**Direct Benefits**:
- **Player recovery**: Faster muscle recovery = better performance
- **Injury prevention**: Proper warm-up/cool-down products reduce injury risk
- **Medical staff satisfaction**: Quality products = better treatment outcomes
- **Cost coverage**: Sponsorship model = free/discounted products

**Intangible Benefits**:
- **Italian market alignment**: Local brand = cultural fit (for Italian teams)
- **Simplicity**: Single supplier for topical recovery = easier procurement
- **Medical staff preference**: If staff prefer Sixtus over alternatives, keep them happy

**Cost Avoidance**:
- Sponsorship deal eliminates €30-60k annual expense
- Products already approved by Italian sports medicine (no trial period)
