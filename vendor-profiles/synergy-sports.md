## Company Overview

**Synergy Sports** is the **industry standard scouting platform** for professional basketball, providing tagged video breakdowns of every possession across multiple leagues (NBA, Euroleague, NCAA, FIBA, domestic leagues). With an estimated **67-78% adoption in Euroleague** (12-14/18 teams), Synergy has achieved **network effects moat**—teams must subscribe because opponents use it, creating scouting arms race.

### Acquisition by Sportradar (2021)

**Sportradar**: Swiss sports data and technology company (€3B+ valuation, publicly traded NASDAQ:SRAD)
- **Portfolio**: Betting data, live odds, broadcast graphics, integrity monitoring, fan engagement
- **Synergy fit**: Adds scouting/analytics to Sportradar's data ecosystem
- **Impact**: Increased investment in AI automation, international expansion, multi-sport integration
- **Market Position**: Featured 18+ times in Global SportsTech Report 2026; European heritage, comprehensive sports data/AI leader

## Technology & Platform

### Core Product: Synergy Sports Platform

**Video Database**:
- **Coverage**: Every game logged and tagged across covered leagues
  - **NBA**: 100% (all 82-game season + playoffs since 2005)
  - **Euroleague**: 100% (all games since ~2007)
  - **NCAA**: 95%+ (Division I men's and women's basketball)
  - **FIBA**: Major tournaments (World Cup, Olympics, EuroBasket)
  - **Domestic leagues**: ACB (Spain), Lega (Italy), BSL (Turkey), others (varies)
- **Total archive**: 2+ million games logged (15+ years of data)

**Play Type Tagging** (Manual + AI):
- **Process**: Video operators (human taggers) or AI classify every possession into play types
- **Play Types**: 
  - **Offensive**: Pick-and-roll ball handler, pick-and-roll roll man, isolation, post-up, spot-up, off-screen, hand-off, transition, cuts, putbacks, misc.
  - **Defensive**: Man-to-man, zone, press, transition defense
- **Granularity**: Player-specific (e.g., "Sergio Llull as pick-and-roll ball handler")
- **Speed**: Manual tagging 12-24 hours post-game; AI tagging real-time (emerging)

**Statistical Breakdowns**:
- **Efficiency**: Points per possession (PPP) by play type
  - **Example**: "Fenerbahçe scores 1.12 PPP on pick-and-roll vs. zone defense (top 10%)"
- **Frequency**: % of possessions by play type
  - **Example**: "Real Madrid runs pick-and-roll 32% of possessions (league average 28%)"
- **Tendencies**: Situational breakdowns (after timeout, out-of-bounds, late clock, late game)
  - **Example**: "Panathinaikos runs horns set 42% after timeout (vs. 18% normally)"
- **Shot Charts**: Location-based shooting % (corner 3s, mid-range, rim, etc.)

**Player Scouting**:
- **Individual profiles**: Every player in database with career stats, play type breakdowns, shot charts
- **Comparison tool**: Compare Player A vs. Player B (head-to-head or vs. league average)
- **Video compilation**: Auto-generate clips (e.g., "All Nikola Mirotic 3-point attempts this season")

## Key Features

### 1. Opponent Scouting (Pre-Game Preparation)

**Workflow**:
1. **Team A** plays **Team B** this week
2. Team A scout logs into Synergy → searches Team B's last 10 games
3. **Offensive tendencies**: Team B runs pick-and-roll 35%, isolation 20%, post-up 15%
4. **Defensive tendencies**: Team B plays drop coverage on ball screens 60%, switch 30%, hedge 10%
5. **Key player**: Team B's star scores 1.25 PPP on isolation (elite) → prepare defensive game plan
6. **ATO plays** (After TimeOut): Team B runs specific plays 60% of the time → anticipate patterns
7. **Video clips**: Export 20-30 video clips of Team B's key plays → share with players in film session

**Output**: Scouting report (PDF or video playlist) for coaches + players

**Time savings**: 
- **Without Synergy**: Watch 10 full games (20 hours), manually track plays in Excel
- **With Synergy**: 2-4 hours (query database, export clips, analyze trends)

### 2. Player Evaluation (Recruitment & Trades)

**Use Case**: Team evaluating free agent or trade target

**Example**: Evaluating **Player X** (shooting guard, age 28, playing in domestic league)

**Synergy Analysis**:
1. **Play type breakdown**: Player X scores 40% of points on spot-up 3s (specialist)
2. **Efficiency**: 1.18 PPP on spot-ups (85th percentile) → elite shooter
3. **Weakness**: 0.72 PPP on isolation (25th percentile) → cannot create own shot
4. **Shot chart**: 42% from corners, 38% above the break, 29% mid-range → 3-and-D archetype
5. **Defensive stats**: Allowed 0.95 PPP when defending isolation (good), 1.08 PPP vs. pick-and-roll (poor)
6. **Video**: Watch 50 possessions → confirm eye test matches stats

**Decision**: Sign Player X as 3-and-D role player (not primary scorer)

**Value**: Data-driven decisions (vs. subjective scouting reports, highlights only)

### 3. Self-Scouting (Improve Own Team)

**Workflow**:
1. Coach reviews own team's Synergy data
2. **Identify weaknesses**: "We're 20th percentile defending pick-and-roll → must improve drop coverage"
3. **Identify strengths**: "We're 90th percentile on transition offense → push pace more"
4. **Optimize play calling**: "Our horns set scores 1.15 PPP (excellent) → run it 20% of possessions instead of 12%"
5. **Practice planning**: Focus drills on weak areas (pick-and-roll defense)

**Feedback loop**: After changes, re-check Synergy stats → validate improvement

## Competitive Positioning

### Synergy vs. Alternatives

**Second Spectrum** (Genius Sports):
- **Type**: AI optical tracking (camera-based player/ball tracking)
- **Strength**: Advanced metrics (spacing, expected possession value, defensive matchups)
- **Weakness**: Expensive (€100-250k/year), requires camera infrastructure, NBA-focused (limited Euroleague coverage)
- **Synergy advantage**: Broader coverage (all Euroleague games logged), lower cost

**Hudl / SportsCode**:
- **Type**: Video analysis tools (manual tagging)
- **Strength**: Customizable (teams tag own videos with own terminology)
- **Weakness**: Manual labor (hours to tag one game), no cross-league database (cannot scout opponents easily)
- **Synergy advantage**: Pre-tagged database (instant access to opponent tendencies)

**InStat** (European Alternative):
- **Type**: Video platform with play-by-play stats
- **Coverage**: European soccer, some basketball
- **Strength**: European focus, lower cost than Synergy (possibly)
- **Weakness**: Less comprehensive basketball coverage, smaller network (fewer teams use it)
- **Synergy advantage**: NBA + Euroleague + NCAA coverage (global database), network effects

**STATS Perform** (Stats LLC):
- **Type**: Advanced stats provider (PER, BPM, on-off stats, SportVU tracking data)
- **Use case**: Front office analytics (player valuation, roster decisions)
- **Synergy advantage**: Video-centric (coaches prefer film), tactical focus (play types, not just box score stats)

**Manual Scouting** (Free):
- **Process**: Watch game film, manually track plays in Excel
- **Strength**: Free (no subscription cost)
- **Weakness**: Time-intensive (20 hours per opponent vs. 3 with Synergy), no comparative data (cannot easily benchmark vs. league average)
- **Synergy advantage**: Efficiency (10x faster) + depth (league-wide comparisons)

## Strengths

1. **Network effects moat**: 67-78% Euroleague adoption → must subscribe to stay competitive
2. **Comprehensive coverage**: Every Euroleague game logged (100% since ~2007), plus NBA, NCAA, FIBA, domestic leagues
3. **Play type tagging**: Standardized taxonomy enables cross-team, cross-league comparisons
4. **Time efficiency**: 3 hours to scout opponent (vs. 20 hours manual)
5. **Historical archive**: 15+ years of data (trend analysis, player development tracking)
6. **Video integration**: Not just stats—actual video clips for film sessions
7. **User familiarity**: Coaches familiar with Synergy from NBA, NCAA, FIBA (consistent interface across leagues)

---

## Weaknesses

1. **Cost**: €15-40k/year prohibitive for budget teams (Partizan, Crvena Zvezda, Žalgiris may struggle)
2. **Manual tagging delays**: 12-24 hours post-game for human taggers (not real-time)
3. **No wearable integration**: Cannot link tactical data (play types) with biomechanical data (load, fatigue)
4. **Limited advanced analytics**: Play types are descriptive (what happened), not predictive (what will happen) or prescriptive (what should happen)
5. **Tagging subjectivity**: Some plays ambiguous (is "dribble handoff into pick-and-roll" classified as handoff or PnR?) → inconsistency possible
6. **Black box algorithm**: Efficiency metrics (PPP) are simple; no sophisticated models like Second Spectrum's expected possession value
7. **Basketball-first, not multi-sport**: Teams with soccer, handball, other sports need separate tools (vs. STATS Perform multi-sport)

## Use Cases & Applications

### Coaching Staff (Pre-Game Scouting)

**Friday Film Session** (Team A plays Team B on Sunday):

1. **Assistant coach** spends Thursday evening on Synergy:
   - Query Team B's last 5 games
   - Export 30 video clips (Team B's most common offensive plays, defensive coverages)
   - Generate PDF report (Team B offensive play type breakdown, shot charts, ATO tendencies)

2. **Friday morning**: Head coach reviews report, creates defensive game plan
   - "Team B runs pick-and-roll 35% → we'll play drop coverage, protect paint"
   - "Team B's Player X shoots 45% on corner 3s → no help-side rotations that leave him open"

3. **Friday afternoon**: Film session with players
   - Watch 30 clips on big screen
   - Coach pauses, explains defensive assignments
   - Players ask questions, mentally prepare

4. **Sunday game**: Team A executes scouted game plan → holds Team B to 0.95 PPP (below their 1.05 average)

**Time investment**: 3-4 hours (vs. 20 hours manual scouting)

### Player Development (Individual Skill Work)

**Example**: Developing young shooting guard

**Synergy analysis**:
- **Current**: Player shoots 32% on catch-and-shoot 3s (below league average 37%)
- **Compare to elite**: NBA players at same position shoot 40%+ → identify gap
- **Video study**: Watch 50 clips of elite shooters (foot positioning, release speed, shot selection)
- **Practice plan**: Focus on shooting form, off-ball movement (get better looks, not contested 3s)
- **Track progress**: Re-check Synergy stats monthly → validate improvement (32% → 35% → 38% over season)

### Front Office (Player Acquisition)

**GM evaluating trade target** (Player Y, age 26, Euroleague):

**Synergy insights**:
- **Offensive role**: 60% spot-up, 25% transition, 10% pick-and-roll, 5% isolation → role player, not creator
- **Efficiency**: 1.12 PPP overall (75th percentile) → above-average scorer
- **Defense**: Opponent scores 1.05 PPP when Player Y is primary defender (45th percentile) → average defender
- **Fit**: Team needs 3-and-D wing → Player Y fits (good shooter, adequate defender)
- **Contract**: Compare stats to similarly-paid players → is €2M/year justified? (Yes, based on Synergy data)

**Decision**: Acquire Player Y

**Alternative without Synergy**: Rely on agent highlights (biased), live scouting (small sample size, subjective)

## Integration with Other Technologies

### Current State (Limited Integration)

**Siloed Data**:
- **Synergy**: Tactical data (play types, shot locations)
- **Wearables**: Biomechanical data (load, fatigue, heart rate)
- **Video tools**: Film breakdowns (Hudl, SportsCode)
- **Analytics platforms**: Statistical models (STATS Perform, custom)

**Problem**: Data in separate systems → cannot correlate tactical demands with fatigue, injury risk
