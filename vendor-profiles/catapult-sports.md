## Product Portfolio

### Catapult Vector (Core Wearable Platform)

**Vector Core**
- **Type**: GPS + IMU wearable pod
- **Form Factor**: Lightweight vest/garment-mounted sensor
- **Data Collected**: GPS position, acceleration, heart rate, PlayerLoad™, high-intensity efforts
- **Sampling Rate**: 10Hz GPS, 100Hz IMU
- **Battery Life**: 8-12 hours
- **Use Case**: Daily training load monitoring
- **Launch**: 2023 refresh of legacy OptimEye platform

**Vector T7** (Basketball-Specific)
- **Type**: Indoor tracking pod (UWB-based)
- **Description**: Purpose-built for basketball without GPS dependency
- **Data Collected**: X/Y positioning, speed, distance, jumps, PlayerLoad, asymmetry
- **Accuracy**: Room-level positioning optimized for court sports
- **Key Feature**: Works indoors where GPS fails
- **Launch**: 2024 specifically for basketball

**Vector S8**
- **Type**: Premium GPS/GNSS pod
- **Description**: Elite-level outdoor tracking
- **Use Case**: Soccer, rugby, American football
- **Not applicable**: Indoor basketball (use T7 instead)

### Catapult Vision (Video Analysis)

**OpenField**
- **Type**: AI-powered tactical video analysis
- **Capabilities**: Automatic player tracking, formation analysis, tactical metrics
- **Integration**: Syncs with Vector wearable data
- **Sports**: Soccer, rugby, basketball
- **Acquisition**: Originally SBG Sports Software, acquired 2018

**Vision for Basketball**
- **Features**: Shot tracking, player positioning, offensive/defensive formations
- **Integration**: Combines video with T7 wearable data
- **Automation**: AI reduces manual tagging time by 80%

### Catapult One (Platform)

**Description**: Unified athlete management system  
**Modules**:
- **Perform**: Training load and wellness monitoring
- **Tactics**: Video analysis and game planning
- **Compete**: Match-day analysis and real-time insights
- **Injury**: Injury tracking and return-to-play protocols

**Key Features**:
- Cloud-based dashboard
- Mobile apps for athletes (wellness check-ins)
- Automated reporting
- Multi-sport support
