## Products & Services

### 1. Official Game Statistics Platform

**Description:**  
Complete live statistical coverage of professional basketball games using trained courtside statisticians and specialized software terminals.

**Technology Components:**
- **Statistician Terminals**: Courtside computers/tablets running Galanis statistics software
- **Real-Time Data Entry**: Trained operators capture all game events live (shots, rebounds, assists, turnovers, fouls, substitutions)
- **Quality Control**: Multi-operator system with cross-validation and post-game review
- **Data Distribution**: Real-time feeds to scoreboards, broadcast graphics, league systems, media platforms
- **Post-Game Reporting**: Official box scores, play-by-play logs, shot charts, team/player statistics

**Statistics Captured:**
- **Basic Stats**: Points, rebounds (offensive/defensive/total), assists, steals, blocks, turnovers, fouls
- **Shooting Stats**: Field goals (made/attempts), 3-point shots, free throws, shooting percentages by zone
- **Advanced Stats**: Plus/minus, possessions, pace, efficiency metrics (calculated from basic stats)
- **Play-by-Play**: Complete game log with timestamps (all scoring plays, fouls, substitutions, timeouts)

**Compliance & Certification:**
- **FIBA Standards**: Meets international basketball statistics standards
- **Euroleague Basketball**: Approved vendor for official Euroleague game statistics
- **League Integration**: Direct integration with league systems for official records

**Use Cases:**
- **Official Records**: Certified statistics for league standings, player awards, historical records
- **Broadcast Integration**: Real-time stats for TV graphics, commentator information, digital platforms
- **Media Relations**: Official box scores distributed to press, journalists, websites, fantasy sports
- **Team Analytics**: Basic game statistics available to coaching staff (though NOT advanced video analysis like Synergy Sports)
- **Fan Engagement**: Live stats displayed on arena video boards, mobile apps, in-game experience

### 2. Instant Replay System

**Description:**  
Courtside video replay equipment and trained operators for referee decision reviews during games.

**Technology Components:**
- **Multi-Camera Integration**: Ingests feeds from arena broadcast cameras (typically 4-8 angles)
- **Replay Control Station**: Courtside terminal with video playback, slow-motion, frame-by-frame controls
- **Referee Interface**: Monitor/screen for referees to review plays during official reviews
- **Operator Services**: Trained Galanis technician operates replay system during games
- **FIBA/Euroleague Compliance**: Meets technical standards for official instant replay systems

**Reviewable Scenarios (per FIBA/Euroleague rules):**
- **Shot Clock Violations**: Did ball contact rim before 24-second expiration?
- **Out-of-Bounds Calls**: Which player last touched ball before going out?
- **2-Point vs. 3-Point Shots**: Was shooter's foot on 3-point line?
- **Flagrant Fouls**: Review severity of contact for ejection decisions
- **End-of-Period Timing**: Did shot release before buzzer?
- **Player Identification**: Which player committed foul in scramble situations?

**Operational Model:**
- **Galanis Operator**: Trained technician runs replay system courtside
- **Referee Request**: Officials signal for replay review to Galanis operator
- **Multi-Angle Review**: Operator cues up relevant camera angles, slow-motion playback
- **Official Decision**: Referee views monitor, makes final ruling
- **Transparency**: Some leagues show replay on arena video board for fan visibility

**Integration:**
- Works with arena broadcast production (camera feeds)
- Separate from broadcast truck (referee-specific replay system)
- No conflict with TV broadcast replay capabilities (independent system for official use)
