## ✅ CONFIRMED Technology Partnerships

### 1. Galanis Sports Data - Official Game Statistics & Instant Replay System

**Category:** Game Operations / Live Statistics / Video Technology  
**Vendor:** Galanis Sports Data (Greece-based sports statistics and technology company)  
**Product:** Official game statistics platform + Instant Replay system

**Confirmation Source:**  
- euroleague-tech-products.md database entry
- **Galanis Sports Data announcement**: Partnership for official game statistics, 2025-26 season

**Technology Details:**  
- **Live Game Statistics**: Real-time capture of all game events (points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, turnovers, fouls, shooting percentages)
- **Official Statisticians**: Galanis-trained personnel operate courtside stats terminals during all Hapoel Tel Aviv home games
- **Data Feed**: Stats distributed to Euroleague Basketball, Israeli League, broadcast partners, media, team staff
- **Instant Replay System**: Courtside video replay equipment for referee reviews (controversial calls, out-of-bounds, shot clock violations, flagrant fouls)
- **Compliance**: Meets Euroleague Basketball and FIBA technical standards for official statistics and replay systems

**Use Cases for Hapoel Tel Aviv**:
1. **Official Game Records**: Certified statistics for all home games (Euroleague + Israeli League) at Drive In Arena
2. **Referee Support**: Instant Replay system enables referees to review critical calls (improves accuracy, reduces controversy)
3. **Broadcast Integration**: Live stats feed to TV broadcasts, arena video boards, league websites, fantasy sports platforms
4. **Media Relations**: Official stats distributed to press, journalists, digital media for game coverage
5. **Team Analytics**: Basic game statistics available to coaching staff (though NOT advanced analytics platform like Synergy Sports)
6. **Fan Experience**: Live stats displayed on arena video boards, mobile apps (in-game engagement)

**Strategic Rationale:**  
- **Euroleague Requirement**: All Euroleague teams must provide certified official statistics and replay systems for home games
- **Israeli Market**: Galanis Sports Data (Greek company) has presence in Israeli basketball market, likely serves multiple Israeli League teams
- **Compliance-Driven**: Technology partnership essential for operational requirements vs. performance enhancement
- **Cost-Effective**: Game operations service (€10-40K/year estimated) vs. expensive performance tech (KINEXON LPS €150-300K/year)

**Technology Scope** (Important Distinction):
- ✅ **Live Game Stats**: Galanis provides real-time officialhome game statistics
- ✅ **Replay System**: Instant Replay hardware/software for referee use
- ❌ **NOT Performance Analytics**: Does NOT provide Synergy Sports-style play-by-play tagging, opponent scouting, or advanced metrics
- ❌ **NOT Wearables**: Galanis does NOT offer player tracking, GPS, or performance monitoring
- **Narrow Scope**: Game operations technology vs. broad performance/analytics platform

**Estimated Investment:**  
- **Instant Replay Equipment**: €10-30K one-time (hardware, installation, software licensing)
- **Annual Service**: €15-40K/year (official statisticians for all home games ~20-30 games/season, software licensing, support)
- **Total Year 1**: €25-70K
- **Ongoing**: €15-40K/year recurring

**Market Context:**  
- Hapoel Tel Aviv one of **first Israeli Euroleague teams** to publicly announce Galanis Sports Data partnership
- Galanis more common in Greek basketball (company HQ in Greece), expanding to Israeli market
- Alternative providers: Genius Sports, IMG Arena, local Israeli statistics operators (Hapoel chose Galanis)
