### 2. KINEXON PERFORM IMU - Inertial Measurement Unit Wearables

**Category:** Performance Tracking / Wearables / Load Monitoring  
**Vendor:** KINEXON Sports & Media GmbH  
**Product:** KINEXON PERFORM IMU

**Confirmation Source:**

- **Metrics Captured**: Player Load (total accelerations), jumps, landings, change of direction, intensity zones, heart rate (if paired with HR strap)
- **Real-Time + Post-Session**: Live monitoring during training, detailed reports after sessions
- **Mobile App**: Players can see personal workload metrics (engagement, accountability)

**Use Cases for Žalgiris:**
1. **Road Game Monitoring**: Track player workload during Euroleague away games (Žalgiris travels across Europe - Athens, Madrid, Barcelona)
2. **Outdoor Training**: Capture data during summer conditioning, beach workouts, outdoor runs
3. **Individual Workouts**: Monitor players' personal training sessions (gym, shooting, off-season)
4. **Injury Rehabilitation**: Track return-to-play progressions (gradually increase load post-injury)
5. **Load Management**: Balance in-arena LPS data with out-of-arena IMU data (total weekly load calculation)
6. **Jump Tracking**: Count vertical jumps per session (knee/ankle injury risk indicator)
7. **Player Accountability**: Mobile app shows players their workload vs. team average (motivation, competition)

### 3. KINEXON COMPETE Vision - AI Shot Tracking & Analysis

**Category:** Video Analysis / Computer Vision / Shooting Analytics  
**Vendor:** KINEXON Sports & Media GmbH  
**Product:** KINEXON COMPETE Vision

**Confirmation Source:**  
- euroleague-tech-products.md database ("KINEXON COMPETE Vision: AI analysis tool that automatically counts and evaluates all shots in training videos")
- KINEXON vendor profile ("COMPETE Vision (2024-25): AI shot tracking launched at Bayern Munich and Žalgiris")
- Part of 2024 comprehensive KINEXON implementation

**Technology Details:**  
- **Computer Vision AI**: Automated shot detection from standard arena/practice cameras (NO special hardware required)
- **Shot Logging**: Automatically counts all shot attempts (makes, misses) during training sessions
- **Shot Location**: Maps shot attempts to court zones (corner 3s, wings, top of key, mid-range, paint)
- **Shot Quality Metrics**: Contested vs. open shots, catch-and-shoot vs. off-dribble, shot distance
- **Shooting Percentage by Zone**: Calculates player efficiency from each court area (identifies strengths/weaknesses)
- **Player Comparison**: Benchmarks individual shooting vs. team/position/Euroleague averages
- **Integration**: Data combines with LPS positioning metrics (e.g., shooting efficiency after high-intensity runs)
- **Coaching Interface**: Dashboard for coaches to review shooting trends, design training drills

**Use Cases for Žalgiris:**
1. **Individual Development**: Identify player shooting weaknesses (e.g., left corner 3-pointer 28% vs. right corner 42%, focus training)
2. **Shot Selection Optimization**: Coaches review shot quality metrics, adjust offensive plays to maximize efficiency
3. **Training Effectiveness**: Track improvement over time (e.g., player increases corner 3-point % from 32% to 38% after focused drills)
4. **Scouting Integration**: Compare Žalgiris player shooting profiles to opponent defenses (exploit mismatches)
5. **Youth Academy**: Practice facility COMPETE Vision tracks youth players, identifies shooting talent early
6. **Pre-Game Preparation**: Review recent shooting trends before games (if player shooting 50%+ from wings recently, exploit)
7. **Fatigue Analysis**: Correlate shooting percentages with LPS-measured workload (does high training load reduce shooting accuracy?)

**Strategic Rationale:**  
- **Shooting Analytics Gap**: Traditional video analysis (Hudl, Synergy) requires manual tagging, COMPETE Vision automates (saves 5-10 hours/week)
- **Offensive Optimization**: Modern basketball emphasizes 3-point shooting efficiency, COMPETE Vision quantifies shot selection quality
- **Andrea Trinchieri Legacy**: Bayern Munich co-developed COMPETE Vision with KINEXON 2024, Trinchieri familiar with platform
- **Euroleague Competition**: Elite clubs use advanced shooting analytics, Žalgiris needs comparable tools to compete
- **Baltic Innovation**: First Lithuanian team with AI shot tracking (competitive advantage vs. regional rivals)

**Investment Analysis:**  
- **Bundled with LPS**: COMPETE Vision included in comprehensive KINEXON suite (no separate licensing fee vs. standalone €30-80K/year)
- **Camera Integration**: Uses existing arena/practice facility cameras (NO additional hardware cost vs. Spiideo €80-150K/year automated cameras)
- **Total Suite Value**: LPS + IMU + COMPETE Vision = €150-300K Year 1, €80-150K/year recurring (all-in-one platform efficiency)
