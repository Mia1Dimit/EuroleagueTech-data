**Category:** Fan Engagement / Content Generation / Social Media

**Confirmation Source:**

1. **Live In-Game Social Media**: During games, WSC Sports auto-publishes highlights to Instagram/Twitter within 20-30 seconds of key plays occurring

2. **Post-Game Recap Videos**: Automatically generates full game highlight reels (2-3 minutes) with all key plays chronologically
   
3. **Social Media Engagement**: 50-100 clips per game (dunks, 3s, blocks) vs. 5-10 manual clips, 2-3x social media engagement increase estimated

**Strategic Rationale:**

- **Labor Efficiency**: Eliminates 1-2 hours post-game manual video editing by media team

- **Euroleague Visibility**: Real-time highlights during games reach international fans (Western Europe primetime 8-10pm CET Euroleague games)
- **June 2020 Adoption**: Early adopter in Euroleague (pre-pandemic investment signals forward-thinking digital strategy)

**Estimated Investment:**
