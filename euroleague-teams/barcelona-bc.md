## ✅ CONFIRMED Technology Partnerships

### Performance Tracking & Wearables

**WIMU PRO (RealTrack Systems)** - Inertial Player Tracking System
- **Product**: WIMU PRO wearable sensors
- **Technology**: Inertial measurement units (IMU) tracking player movement indoors and outdoors
- **Data Captured**: Distances, acceleration, deceleration, leaps, change of direction, workload metrics
- **Use Cases**:
  - Training session monitoring (indoor practice courts and outdoor conditioning)
  - Load management and injury prevention
  - Player development tracking
  - Session intensity quantification
- **Users**: Athletes, coaches, physiotherapists, strength & conditioning staff
- **Source**: Referenced in euroleague-tech-products.md and vendor market analysis
- **Estimated Investment**: €30-80K/year (Spanish vendor, competitive pricing vs. Catapult/STATSports)

**Key Advantages for Barcelona**:
- Spanish company (RealTrack Systems based in Almería, Spain) - local support, language, cultural fit
- Cost-effective alternative to Catapult/STATSports (40-60% lower pricing)
- Indoor-optimized (no GPS dependency, works in Palau Blaugrana practice courts)
- Basketball-validated technology (used by Spanish ACB Liga Endesa clubs)

---

**STATSports** - GPS-Based Player Tracking (Noted)
- **Product**: APEX GPS system, Sonra analytics platform
- **Status**: ⚠️ Referenced in tracking lists but implementation details unclear
- **Technology**: GPS + accelerometer + gyroscope wearable pods
- **Potential Use**: Outdoor training sessions, pre-season conditioning camps
- **Note**: May be used in conjunction with WIMU PRO (GPS for outdoor, WIMU for indoor) or replaced by WIMU system
