# Fenerbahçe Beko Istanbul - Technology Profile

## Team Overview

**Full Name:** Fenerbahçe Spor Kulübü Basketball  
**Common Name:** Fenerbahçe Beko Istanbul  
**Founded:** 1913 (first basketball club in Ottoman Empire)  
**Location:** Istanbul, Turkey  
**Home Arena:** Ülker Sports and Event Hall (13,800 capacity)  
**Arena Opened:** January 25, 2012  
**Current Head Coach:** Šarūnas Jasikevičius  
**Athletic Trainer:** İlker Belgutay  
**Website:** https://www.fenerbahce.org/branches/menbasketball

**Recent Success:**
- **2 × EuroLeague Champions** (2016-17, 2024-25)
- **Triple Crown Winners** (2024-25 season: EuroLeague + Turkish Super League + Turkish Cup - first Turkish team to achieve this)
- **15 × Turkish Championships**
- **10 × Turkish Cups**
- **4 consecutive EuroLeague Final Four appearances** (2014-2019)

**Market Significance:**
- Istanbul-based club with massive fanbase (one of Turkey's "big three" sports clubs)
- Only Turkish team to win multiple EuroLeague titles
- Historic 2024-25 season: Second EuroLeague title + Triple Crown achievement
- Defeated NBA teams: Boston Celtics (2012, at Ülker Arena 97-91) and Brooklyn Nets (2015, at Barclays Center 101-96 - first Turkish team to beat NBA team in USA)

## Technology Partnerships

### ⭐ CONFIRMED PARTNERSHIPS

#### **1. KINEXON PERFORM IMU**
**Category:** Performance Tracking - Wearable Sensors  
**Deployment:** Training load monitoring  
**Source:** [vendor-profiles/kinexon.md](../vendor-profiles/kinexon.md) database entry  
**Implementation Details:**
- **Technology:** Inertial measurement unit (IMU) sensors for player biomechanics
- **Sampling Rate:** 100+ Hz data collection
- **Metrics:** Acceleration, deceleration, jump height, landing forces, change of direction
- **Use Case:** Training load management and injury prevention
- **Status:** Active deployment (confirmed in KINEXON vendor profile)

**Context:** Fenerbahçe uses KINEXON's wearable sensor technology to monitor player workload during training sessions. This is particularly important for managing the demanding EuroLeague + domestic league schedule (82+ games per season including Turkish league). The IMU sensors help strength & conditioning staff like İlker Belgutay track accumulated fatigue and adjust training intensity.

## Ülker Sports and Event Hall - Facility Technology

**Arena Details:**
- **Capacity:** 13,800 (includes 11,870 fan seats + VIP/lounge areas)
- **Opened:** January 25, 2012 (inaugural game: Fenerbahçe vs. EA7 Emporio Armani Milano)
- **Operator:** Anschutz Entertainment Group (AEG)
- **Total Area:** 55,000 m² (590,000 sq ft)

**Confirmed Technology Features:**
- **Scoreboard:** LED 250 m² (2,700 sq ft), weighing 28.5 tons - **largest scoreboard in Europe**
- **Locker Rooms:** 6 main locker rooms + 5 additional small locker rooms (supports multi-sport usage)
- **Training Facilities:** 2,500 capacity practice hall (used for training and reserve team games)
- **Hospitality:** 44 lounge areas + dedicated VIP seating sections

**Fenerbahçe Basketball Museum:**
- **Opened:** November 18, 2022
- **Significance:** First and only basketball museum in Turkey
- **Exhibits:** Team history from 1913 to present, all trophies including 2017 EuroLeague championship, retired jerseys (#6 Mirsad Türkcan, #7 Ömer Onan, #X İbrahim Kutluay)

**Arena Infrastructure (No Advanced Court Technology Confirmed):**
- **NO ASB GlassFloor:** Standard hardwood court (no LED floor technology like Panathinaikos or Bayern Munich)
- **NO publicly disclosed court sensors:** Unlike some Euroleague arenas with embedded LPS systems
- **WiFi/Connectivity:** Modern arena with free WiFi for fans (standard for 2012+ construction)
