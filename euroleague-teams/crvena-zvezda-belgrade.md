## ✅ CONFIRMED Technology Partnerships

### Fan Engagement & Digital Authentication

**Authena** - Digital Authentication Platform for Game Jerseys
- **Product**: Blockchain/NFC authentication system
- **Technology**: NFC tags embedded in player-worn game jerseys linked to unique NFTs
- **Implementation**: Each jersey worn by players includes NFC tag paired with digital NFT
- **Use Cases**:
  - Jersey authentication (proving official merchandise vs. counterfeits)
  - "Phygital" collectibles (physical jersey + digital NFT ownership)
  - Fan engagement (fans can verify jersey authenticity via mobile app)
  - Memorabilia value (game-worn jerseys have verifiable provenance via blockchain)
- **Target Users**: Fans, collectors, merchandise buyers, team executives
- **Source**: Referenced in euroleague-tech-products.md
- **Estimated Investment**: €10-30K/year (implementation + per-jersey tagging fees)

**Strategic Rationale**:
- Red Star has passionate fanbase (historically one of largest in Europe)
- Serbian market has counterfeit merchandise issues (Authena combats fakes)
- NFT integration appeals to younger, tech-savvy fans
- Memorabilia sales important revenue stream for budget-constrained club

**Technology Context**:
- Authena is sports-focused blockchain company (Nike, Adidas partners)
- Red Star among early European basketball adopters of jersey authentication
- Basketball division leverages technology less common in Euroleague (most teams focus on performance tech)
