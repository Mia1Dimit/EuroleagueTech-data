## ✅ CONFIRMED Technology Partnerships

### Performance Tracking & Wearables
**KINEXON Sports** - Basketball Analytics Partnership
- **Confirmed**: Official press release
- **Source**: https://kinexon-sports.com/pr/kinexon-sports-announces-basketball-analytics-partnership-with-euroleague-team-panathinaikos-bc-aktor/
- **Technology**: LPS (Local Positioning System) tracking
- **Capabilities**: Real-time player positioning, speed, distance, tactical analytics
- **Use Cases**: Training load monitoring, game analytics, injury prevention

### Facilities Technology
**ASB GlassFloor** - Interactive LED Court
- **Confirmed**: Public installation (2024)
- **Venue**: OAKA Arena
- **Technology**: 450,000 LED interactive playing surface
- **Investment**: Not publicly disclosed (industry estimates €1-2M)
- **Use Cases**: Pre-game shows, sponsor activations, halftime entertainment, tactical training
- **Industry Recognition**: Featured in Global SportsTech Report 2026 (p. 81)
  - Listed alongside FC Bayern Munich Basketball as European basketball teams adopting advanced LED court technology
  - NBA acquired equity stake in ASB GlassFloor, validating technology
  - FIBA Level 1 certified

### Game Operations Technology
**Galanis Sports Data** - Official Statistics & Instant Replay
- **Confirmed**: Company website portfolio page (galanissportsdata.com/portfolio/euroleague-greece/)
- **Project**: "Euroleague Greece" - Official statistics services for Greek Euroleague teams since 2016
- **Duration**: 2016-present (8+ years confirmed operation)
- **Services**:
  - **Official Game Statistics**: Real-time statistical coverage of all Panathinaikos Euroleague home games (points, rebounds, assists, shooting, play-by-play)
  - **TV Graphics**: Live broadcast graphics for Nova Sports Production (Greek broadcaster)
  - **Instant Replay System**: Installation and operation of courtside video replay equipment for referee decision reviews
  - **Secretariat Support**: Electronic Match Report and game operations assistance
- **Technology**: Courtside statistician terminals, multi-camera replay control station, data distribution feeds
- **Vendor Profile**: Greek sports data company (founded 1984, 300+ experts, 40-year track record including Athens 2004 and Beijing 2008 Olympics)
- **Partnership Context**: Galanis also provides same services for Olympiacos B.C. Euroleague home games (all Greek Euroleague teams covered)
- **Estimated Cost**: €25-60K/year (statistician services, replay operations, software licensing)
- **Category**: Compliance technology (required for Euroleague home games) vs. performance technology (optional analytics/tracking)
