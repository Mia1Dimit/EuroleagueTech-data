## Technology Partnerships

### ⭐ CONFIRMED PARTNERSHIPS

#### **1. KINEXON PERFORM Comprehensive Suite**
**Category:** Performance Tracking - LPS + Wearable Sensors + AI Vision  
**Deployment:** Full ecosystem implementation since 2018  
**Source:** [vendor-profiles/kinexon.md](../vendor-profiles/kinexon.md) database entry + KINEXON Munich headquarters proximity

**Technologies Deployed:**

**a) KINEXON PERFORM LPS (Local Positioning System)**
- **Technology:** UWB (Ultra-Wideband) radio sensors installed in BMW Park and SAP Garden
- **Accuracy:** Centimeter-level player tracking (± 5cm)
- **Sampling Rate:** Real-time updates at 25+ Hz
- **Metrics:** Player speed, distance covered, positioning, spacing, off-ball movement
- **Use Case:** Advanced tactical analysis, player development, game preparation

**b) KINEXON PERFORM IMU (Inertial Measurement Units)**
- **Technology:** Wearable sensors (jerseys or chest straps)
- **Sampling Rate:** 100+ Hz motion data
- **Metrics:** Acceleration, deceleration, jumps, landing forces, change of direction stress
- **Use Case:** Training load management, injury prevention (particularly important for 82+ game season: BBL + EuroLeague)

**c) KINEXON COMPETE Vision (AI-Powered Video Analysis)**
- **Technology:** Automated tracking from broadcast camera feeds
- **Launch:** 2024-25 EuroLeague season (new product)
- **Capabilities:** Player/ball tracking without physical sensors, automated event detection
- **Use Case:** Opponent scouting from Euroleague game footage, home game tracking redundancy

**d) SAP Sports One Integration**
- **Technology:** Enterprise data platform connecting KINEXON data with team analytics
- **Provider:** Co-developed by KINEXON + SAP (both Munich-based companies)
- **Unique Feature:** Bayern Munich is a KINEXON + SAP integration showcase (both companies are major sponsors)
- **Capabilities:** Dashboard for coaches, centralized performance database, historical trend analysis

**Implementation Timeline:**
- **2018:** Initial KINEXON deployment (LPS + IMU at BMW Park/Audi Dome)
- **2024:** SAP Garden opening → LPS installation in new arena
- **2024-25:** COMPETE Vision added (first EuroLeague season with AI vision product)

**Strategic Context:**
- **Munich Cluster:** KINEXON HQ in Munich (10km from BMW Park), enabling close technical partnership
- **Corporate Alignment:** SAP is major FC Bayern sponsor (naming rights to SAP Garden), creating natural integration opportunity
- **Elite Tier:** Comprehensive suite (LPS + IMU + Vision + SAP) makes Bayern one of most tech-advanced teams in European basketball
- **5+ Years Operational:** Longest-running KINEXON deployment in Euroleague (since 2018), indicating mature implementation

## Dual-Arena Strategy - Technology Implications

### **BMW Park** (Domestic Arena)
**Capacity:** 6,700  
**Opened:** 1972 (as Rudi-Sedlmayer-Halle for 1972 Munich Olympics basketball)  
**Renovations:** 2011 (modernized as Audi Dome), 2023 (renamed BMW Park)  
**Primary Use:** Majority of BBL regular season games (lower-demand matchups)  
**Technology:**
- ✅ **ASB GlassFloor LED Court** (confirmed)
- ✅ **KINEXON LPS installed** (arena sensors for player tracking)
- Standard scoreboard, broadcast infrastructure

**Historic Significance:**
- Original 1972 Olympics basketball venue (Rudi-Sedlmayer-Halle)
- 52-year history as Munich's primary basketball facility
- Investment in ASB GlassFloor shows commitment to legacy venue premium experience despite age

---

### **SAP Garden** (EuroLeague Arena)
**Capacity:** 11,500 (basketball)  
**Opened:** September 27, 2024 (inaugural game: Buffalo Sabres vs. Red Bull Munich ice hockey exhibition)  
**Location:** Olympic Park Munich (next to Olympiastadion, 1972 Summer Olympics main venue)  
**Operator:** Red Bull GmbH (owner of EHC Red Bull Munich ice hockey team)  
**Shared Tenant:** EHC Red Bull Munich (DEL ice hockey) and Bayern Munich Basketball  
**Construction Cost:** €110 million

**Technology Infrastructure:**
- ✅ **KINEXON LPS installed** (sensors integrated during 2024 construction)
- ✅ **Free WiFi for fans** (standard modern arena feature)
- ✅ **Mobile Order app integration** (concessions ordering from seats)
- **Hospitality:** 11 executive suites, premium seating areas
- **Architecture:** 18 lattice girders (170 tonnes each), green roof with 8,000 m² flower meadow
- **Multi-Purpose:** Convertible for basketball, ice hockey, concerts (12,500 capacity for events)

**Basketball-Specific Features:**
- Dedicated basketball floor (standard hardwood, NOT LED court like BMW Park)
- 11,500 capacity makes it one of largest Euroleague basketball arenas in 2024-25 season
- KINEXON LPS tracking for Euroleague games (opponent scouting data collection)

**Strategic Rationale for Dual Arenas:**
- **Demand-Based Usage:** SAP Garden (11,500) for high-demand Euroleague games, BMW Park (6,700) for BBL midweek games
- **Revenue Optimization:** Avoid underselling tickets for low-demand games in oversized arena
- **Facility Differentiation:** LED court experience at BMW Park (fan engagement innovation), premium hospitality at SAP Garden (corporate sales)
- **Technology Testing:** BMW Park serves as innovation lab (ASB GlassFloor trials) before potential SAP Garden deployment
