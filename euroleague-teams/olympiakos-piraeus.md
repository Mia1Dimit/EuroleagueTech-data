## Executive Summary

**Olympiakos Piraeus** is one of the most successful Greek basketball clubs with **3 EuroLeague titles** and fierce rivalry with Panathinaikos Athens. Unlike Italian Euroleague teams, Olympiakos shows **selective public disclosure** of technology partnerships.

**Key Finding**: **2 confirmed technology partnerships** 
1. **Evo4HP ArtemYs** (performance tracking/injury prevention - confirmed via industry sources, NOT on sponsor page)
2. **Galanis Sports Data** (official statistics + instant replay - confirmed via vendor website, Euroleague Greece project since 2016)

**Transparency Pattern**: Olympiakos uses technology but has mixed disclosure practices (Evo4HP confidential, Galanis publicly listed by vendor)

## Confirmed Technology Partnerships

### 1. **Evo4HP** - Performance Tracking & Injury Prevention

**Category**: Performance Tracking, Biomechanics Analysis, Injury Prevention  
**Product**: **ArtemYs** - AI-powered myoskeletal monitoring platform  
**Status**: ✅ CONFIRMED (via industry sources, March 2026)  
**Public Disclosure**: ❌ NOT on team sponsor pages

**Technology Details**:
- **Type**: Wearable IMU sensors (200 Hz sampling, 15g detection range)
- **Capabilities**:
  - Personalized workload monitoring with athlete-specific zones
  - **Muscle-specific stress load tracking** (7+ major muscle groups per leg)
  - **Muscle capacity monitoring** (real-time fatigue detection)
  - **5-day predictive forecasting** (workload/injury risk prediction)
  - Bilateral asymmetry detection
  - AI-generated Ground Reaction Forces
  - Digital twin creation for each athlete

**Use Case**:
- **Injury prevention**: Early detection of muscle micro-failures before they become major injuries
- **Load management**: Personalized training intensity adjustments based on muscle capacity
- **Recovery monitoring**: Muscle-by-muscle capacity tracking (e.g., Gastrocnemius L 101% vs. R 90%)
- **Euroleague schedule management**: Predictive analytics help plan training around packed fixture schedules

**Greek Connection**:
- **Vendor HQ**: Greece (Athens-based startup)
- **Local advantage**: Greek-speaking support, cultural fit, proximity for on-site visits
- **Founder**: Dr. Sofia Pomakidou (PhD in Bioengineering)

**Estimated Investment**: €40-100k+/year (depending on tier: Standard/Pro/Elite)

**Competitive Context**:
- **Panathinaikos uses**: Kinexon PERFORM IMU (via Conartia)
- **Olympiakos choice**: Selected Greek innovator Evo4HP over German Kinexon
- **Market positioning**: Evo4HP specializes in muscle injury prevention (vs. Kinexon's broader performance tracking)

**Why Not Publicly Announced**:
- **Business model**: Olympiakos does not monetize technology partnerships via sponsorships
- **Vendor policy**: Evo4HP respects client confidentiality (no public client list on website)
- **Competitive secrecy**: Teams may prefer to keep performance advantages private

### 2. **Galanis Sports Data** - Official Statistics & Instant Replay

**Category**: Game Operations Technology, Official Statistics, Referee Replay Systems  
**Product**: **Statistical Coverage + Instant Replay System** for Euroleague home games  
**Status**: ✅ CONFIRMED (via company website, March 2026)  
**Public Disclosure**: ✅ Listed on Galanis portfolio page (galanissportsdata.com/portfolio/euroleague-greece/)

**Technology Details**:
- **Type**: Game operations technology (NOT performance tracking)
- **Services**:
  - **Official Game Statistics**: Real-time statistical coverage of all Olympiakos Euroleague home games (points, rebounds, assists, shooting, play-by-play)
  - **TV Graphics**: Live broadcast graphics for Nova Sports Production (Greek broadcaster partnership)
  - **Instant Replay System**: Courtside video replay equipment and trained operators for referee decision reviews
  - **Secretariat Support**: Electronic Match Reports and game operations assistance
- **Technology**: Courtside statistician terminals, multi-camera replay control station, real-time data distribution feeds
- **Compliance**: FIBA/Euroleague certified official statistics provider

**Project Timeline**:
- **Duration**: Since 2016 (8+ years confirmed operation)
- **Project Name**: "Euroleague Greece" - covers ALL Greek Euroleague teams' home games
- **Partner Teams**: Olympiakos + Panathinaikos Athens (both Greek Euroleague clubs)

**Vendor Profile**:
- **Company**: Galanis Sports Data S.A. (Greek sports data company)
- **Founded**: 1984 (40+ years in business)
- **Headquarters**: Agia Paraskevi, Athens, Greece
- **Employees**: 300+ experts
- **Track Record**: 67,000+ games covered, 1,300+ major events, Athens 2004 Olympics, Beijing 2008 Olympics official statistics provider
- **Contact**: +30 210 6770901, info@galanissportsdata.com

**Broadcast Integration**:
- **Nova Sports Production**: Long-term partnership with Greek broadcaster for live TV graphics
- **TV Collaboration**: Galanis provides real-time statistics feeds for broadcast commentators and on-screen graphics during Euroleague home games

**Use Case**:
- **Compliance Requirement**: Euroleague mandates official statistics and instant replay systems for all home games
- **Referee Support**: Instant replay system enables controversial call reviews (shot clock violations, out-of-bounds calls, 2pt vs 3pt shots, flagrant fouls, end-of-period timing)
- **Broadcast Quality**: Live stats enhance TV viewer experience (on-screen graphics, commentator information)
- **Official Records**: Statistics feed to Euroleague Basketball for standings, player awards, historical records

**Estimated Investment**: €25-60K/year (statistician services + replay operations + software licensing)

**Technology Distinction** (Critical):
- **Galanis = Game Operations**: Official statistics for compliance and broadcast (required technology)
- **Evo4HP = Performance Technology**: Athlete monitoring for injury prevention (optional competitive advantage)
- **Different Categories**: Teams need BOTH compliance tech (Galanis) AND performance tech (Evo4HP, KINEXON, etc.)

**Competitive Context**:
- **Panathinaikos uses**: Galanis Sports Data (same provider - Euroleague Greece project covers both clubs)
- **Olympiakos + Panathinaikos**: Shared service provider for Euroleague home games (Greek teams unified under one statistics vendor)
- **Why Greek Provider**: Home market advantage (Athens-based company), Nova Sports collaboration, 40-year Greek sports data heritage

**Why Publicly Disclosed (Unlike Evo4HP)**:
- **Vendor Portfolio**: Galanis publicly lists "Euroleague Greece" project on company website (client consent obtained)
- **Broadcast Visibility**: Nova Sports collaboration means partnership visible to Greek TV viewers
- **Less Competitive Sensitivity**: Official statistics required for all teams (no competitive advantage to hide)
- **EU Funding Transparency**: Galanis received ESPA 2021-2027 grant (government support, public disclosure requirement)
